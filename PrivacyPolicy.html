PRIVACY & COOKIE POLICY
Respecting Your Privacy
Our respect for our employees' right to security of their personal information is paramount. We have adopted this privacy policy ("Policy") which provides you with information about the kind of personal information which we may collect from you, how we handle such information and the rights which you have to control our use of your personal data. Your personal data collected by us shall be used by us strictly in accordance with applicable Indian Laws.
This Policy sets out
Our practices and policies on the management of personal information of our employees collected by us.
Type of personal or sensitive personal data or information collected.
The purpose of collection of personal information, the manner of collection, storage, use of and disclosure of such personal information; and Security practices and procedures adopted by us to protect your personal information collected by us.
We request you to read this Policy carefully before sharing personal information with us. By visiting Tatastrive.com, we assume your acceptance to the practices described in this Policy and if you have any questions please contact us at STRIVE@tatasustainability.com
What personal information do we collect and store
We shall collect your personal information if it is reasonable and necessary to do so, subject to this Policy and applicable Indian law.
We collect information in the following ways
Information you give us. For example, when you upload any information about yourself like your name, email address, house/office address and telephone number in order to apply for job vacancies in Tata STRIVE. You also provide certain information when you create or update your profile on Tatastrive.com. Some of this information is visible to other Tatastrive.com subscribers.
Information we get from your use of Tatastrive.com. This information includes, passwords, device information, location information, Cookies and anonymous identifiers. Information from Other Sources: We may also collect personal information about you from other third parties, such as third party service providers, whose websites you access through Tatastrive.com and with whom you share any information.
We may also gather certain information automatically and store it in log files. For example, this information may include Internet Protocol (IP) addresses or other device identifiers, browser information, cookies Internet Service Provider (ISP), operating system, location, date/time stamp and clickstream data.
We take reasonable measures to ensure that your personal information is protected from unauthorised access, loss, misuse, disclosure or alteration.
Access to your personal information
If you need to update your personal information in our records (e.g. if you change your address), please provide the updated details at the relevant place as stated above, to enable us to make the change. You may be requested to provide some verification before the information is recorded. We shall not be responsible for the authenticity of any personal information supplied to us by you.
How we share your information
You may be accessing several external links while navigating through Tatastrive.com. These external links may belong to Tata companies or third party service providers. We may share personal information with other Tata companies, and also with other third parties.
Subject to the following terms
Employee consent: We will share your personal information with other Tata companies, and also with other third parties only with your consent. You can indicate your consent by using the website and any services through the website.
Same level of data protection: We shall share your personal information only with such companies, organisations and individuals that ensure the same level of data protection that is set out under this policy and as required by applicable Indian laws.
Subject to the terms mentioned above, we would share your personal information with a third party for following reasons and under the following circumstances
For external processing: Provide your personal information to our affiliates or other trusted business associates or technology partners to process it for us, based on our instructions and in compliance with this Policy and applicable laws.
Third-Party Service Providers: We employ other companies and individuals to perform certain functions on our behalf. They have access to your personal information needed to perform their functions, but shall not use it for other purposes or disclose it further.
Promotional Offers and contests: Sometimes we post offers and host Contests on Tatastrive.com on behalf of different Tata companies. We may share some of your details with the company to enable fulfilment of the offer or contest.
For legal reasons: We will share your personal information if the same is necessary to: meet any applicable law, regulation, legal process or enforceable governmental request, enforce applicable Terms of Service, including investigation of potential violations, detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security or technical issues, protect against harm to the rights, property or safety of us, our users or the public as required or permitted by law.
Please note that we can share your personal information, without your consent, with Government agencies upon receiving a written request from any Government agency for such information.
Information security
We strive to protect ourselves and our users from unauthorised access to or unauthorised alteration, disclosure or destruction of information we hold.
Grievance Cell
If you have any concerns regarding:
Discrepancies between the personal information provided by you and the information reflected in our records and the same have not been corrected and/or updated after you have provided updated information along with any verification required; or in respect of processing of your personal information; then you may contact STRIVE@tatasustainability.com
Your concern shall be addressed in a time-bound manner.
Use of Cookies
The site may use cookies or web beacons to store personal information, only for the purpose of updating user preferences and to ensure fast loading of pages. Also hit counters and metres maybe used for statistical information and web reports.
Changes
Our Policy may change from time to time. We will not reduce your rights under this Policy without your explicit consent. We will post any changes or update to this Policy on this webpage and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice. We will also keep prior versions of this Policy in an archive for your review.
When this Policy applies
The Policy applies to all of the services offered by Tatastrive.com. This Policy does not apply to services offered by other companies or individuals, or other sites linked from Tatastrive.com, and which have separate privacy policies that do not incorporate this Policy. For example, the external websites which you may access through Tatastrive.com and which have their own privacy policies.
