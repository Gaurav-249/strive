Tata STRIVE extension centres are means of extending the reach of Tata STRIVE programmes into various locations. Our Tata STRIVE extension centres are typically smaller than TSSDCs, focusing on fewer trades catering to local demand. Like with TSSDCs, Tata STRIVE manages the operations of these centres as well. Extension centres are run in premises leased by Tata STRIVE or in premises of our partners including educational institutions, government or private enterprises.
