Phygital Skilling Model of Tata STRIVE
Phygital is a unique skilling model that makes possible quality job-oriented courses available to Indiabs youth wherever they are.
The Phygital model has been designed:
For scale and democratisation of access.
To address the current ecosystem challenges of lack of quality content.
To provide access to quality trainers.
Standardise assessments & certifications.
Increase industry and job linkages.
Tata STRIVE is a flagship CSR programme from the Tata Group that has invested in developing top-notch, standardised:
Domain content.
Unique employability skills training.
Immersive faculty training.
Personalised tech-enabled counselling processes.

Which are capable of powerful impact when delivered to its high standards.

Assessments are digital and uniform
across the country, successful
candidates earn a Tata STRIVE
certificate. The entire course is
seamlessly available through the
TCS iON platform developed by
our technology partner TCS.
It is fully supported by Tata STRIVEbs
intensive faculty training, extensive
industry connect and TCS iON
networks across the country.



If you are a Training Provider, then like Tata STRIVE, you too have a vision to reach millions of youth and make a visible impact. By joining hands with Tata STRIVE we can together create a meta-skill development network that delivers high-quality training to thousands of youth across 100 cities of the country.
Starting with few courses, we will continually add more job-oriented courses in the future. Phygital is the future of learning and the future is just around the corner.
