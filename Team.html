TEAM

Anita Rajan
Chief Executive Officer,
Tata STRIVE
 view profile

Anita Rajan is the Chief Executive Officer of Tata STRIVE and Vice President, Tata Community Initiatives Trust since 2015. Tata STRIVE is a Skills Building initiative of the Tata Trusts and has established itself as a leader in the Skill Development sector in India. Over 4 lakh people have been impacted through the skilling development work of the Tata Trusts.
Anita was deeply involved with the Indian National Skilling Ecosystem during its formative years having worked with the Advisor to Prime Minister on Skill Development as his Chief of Staff and later with the Chairman, National Skill Development Corporation
Anita has over thirty years of diverse experience in the education and management sectors. She has been with the Tata Group for 17 years. Anita is an active member of Industry bodies, she is the Co-chair of CII CSR committee (Western Region) and an active representative of Industry as a speaker at seminars in India and overseas.
Prior to Tata STRIVE, Anita was with Tata Consultancy Services for 13 years, where she worked closely with the CEO on numerous initiatives of the company; including supporting the publication of a bestselling book on the company. Prior to TCS she was head of Research and Training at Schoolnet India, an e-education company that aimed to leverage technology in education. Earlier she worked with the advertising firm Lintas in advertising research. Her corporate career was preceded by a decade of teaching in High Schools across India.
Her academic credentials include an MBA in Marketing from Sydenham Institute of Management Studies, Mumbai, she is an alumnus of the prestigious St Stephens College, Delhi University where she earned a BA Maths Honours degree. She also holds a Bachelors in Education from Mumbai University.

Ameya Vanjari
Head – Operations, Partnerships
& Technology - Tata STRIVE
view profile

Ameya is Head of Operations, Partnerships and Technology at Tata STRIVE. In this role, reporting to the CEO of Tata STRIVE, he is responsible for leading the organization - defining the strategic priorities, plans, facilitating governance, and steering the team towards the goals set.
Before this, he was heading technology and Innovation at STRIVE, where he was responsible for shaping digital capabilities to deliver value for the organization and its ecosystem. He established and led STRIDEA - an initiative to establish a culture of innovation within the organization. Some innovations being piloted include artificial intelligence-based gamified mobile assessments, virtual reality solutions for audits, chatbot-based learning and flipped classrooms.
Prior to this, Ameya led the Business & IT Architecture Consulting practice at TCS. In that role, he worked with business leaders, CIOs, and CDOs across the globe to define strategies and shape initiatives to deliver business value enabled by digital and information technologies. As a consulting practice leader, he established the unit and grew the practice to a global team of over 150 consultants working at the intersection of business and technology.
Ameya has also been a CIO for a retail enterprise as part of a consulting engagement he led and delivered.
Passionate in the domains of technology, education and design, he has a keen interest in the evolution of enterprises and society. He has deep experience in retail, banking, financial services, and education industries. He has global exposure by traveling and working with customers in over 15 countries.
Ameya holds a degree in engineering in Electronics from V.J.T.I, Mumbai and has done a General Management programme from Iacocca Institute, Lehigh University, PA, US.
Photography and sports - especially cricket, are his passions apart from food and technology.

Ajita Karve
Principal Lead – Design
& Quality- Tata STRIVE
view profile

Ajita is a part of the Design and Quality team at Tata STRIVE. Conceptualizing and designing programmes using technology, new learning models for domain and life skills training, entrepreneurship, defining processes, impact assessment metrics, developing and facilitating competency building solutions and Quality Assessment framework/s - a few of the areas that she works with.
She led the Learning and Development function for two Tata organizations before moving on to Tata STRIVE. She had been instrumental in designing and deploying learning strategies and solutions with a business focus, developing, and managing talent, building competency frameworks & assessment centres, leadership development interventions & behavioral training programmes, managing learning effectiveness and impact. Additionally, she also has a rich experience in managing global projects for developing state of the art technology-based learning content, instructional design, delivering training & OD interventions.
She has a Bachelor’s degree in Commerce from the University of Mumbai and a Master’s degree in Business Administration (HRM) from Southern New Hampshire University.
She is a certified assessor of the Tata Business Excellence Model and a certified Quality Assessor and Counsellor from (QCI) NABET.

 view profile

For nearly two decades, Bijitha has helped organisations work towards implementing large scale projects with a quality mindset. She has been with the Tata Group for more than a decade and also has an entrepreneurial stint of 5 years.

Raghu Reddy
Principal Lead – Technology
Tata STRIVE
 view profile

Raghu leads the Technology and Innovation function at Tata STRIVE and is responsible for planning and implementing technology solutions to support the capabilities and processes at Tata STRIVE. This entails introducing various innovations for the programme to facilitate development of youth for success in the future; enabling developments of tomorrow's skills while maintaining quality.
He has 25 years of international experience – across all continents – multiple domains and technologies and is an expert in architecture consulting, project management & leadership, process modeling, process improvements.
Raghu has executed various projects via managing strategic IT planning, integrating technology-based activities into the corporate strategy through effective relationships with key people (CXO team) throughout the company, overseeing IT performance as well as the development and maintenance of the IT strategic plan; approval of vendors and monitoring their financial condition; approval and monitoring of major projects, IT budgets, organizational priorities, standards, procedures, and overall IT performance; including, the quintessential review of the adequacy and allocation of IT resources in terms of funding, personnel, equipment, and service levels.
Raghu holds a Masters in Industrial Engineering from PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. He is also Togaf-certified Enterprise Architect.
In his free time, he loves to learn and apply new technologies and also has a penchant for solving puzzles and spotting patterns.
